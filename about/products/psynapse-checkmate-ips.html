Psynape Checkmate IPS

The Checkmate Intrusion Protection System is a next-generation network security product that applies innovative behavior-based methodologies to detect malicious activities before the user has the opportunity to inflict harm. Surpassing traditional intrusion detection, intrusion prevention and hybrid systems, Checkmate is the only intrusion protection system that can anticipate a hacker's next move.

Experience highlights

Between 1999 and 2004, I was the CTO of Psynapse Technologies, a Washington, DC technology startup, working to develop a behavioral-based network intrusion protection system. We received the initial funding of $11M from DARPA and the US Navy to develop the technology and create a commercial offering.

As the Chief Architect, I recruited and led a team of high-performing hardware, networking, and application developers to take the idea and concept of applying neural networks in a networking application and develop the Checkmate Intrusion Protection System (IPS) network appliance.

Review this on-location episode of World Business Review by the former US Secretary of State General Alexander Haig to learn more about Psynapse Technologies and Checkmate IPS.
